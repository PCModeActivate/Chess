Instructions

Chess is a two-player game. In this instance of modified chess,
both players will play on the same computer, and control the
pieces using the same mouse.

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chess

Controls:
    Left click on a piece that is on the current player's side to select it
    Click on the empty space or another piece, and the piece will be moved there.
    Click on the same piece again to unselect it
    Once the piece is moved, pass the mouse to the other player and let them select one of their own pieces.
    Have fun!
